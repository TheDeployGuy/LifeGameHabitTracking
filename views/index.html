Game of Life

So what is "The Game of Life System" for habit tracking?
It's a very simple grid system that is used to track your habits on a weekly basis.

So how does it work?

It starts by having you identify three good habits you want to build

You then assign each habit a difficulty rating from 1-5. So how hard is this habit for you to do the harder an activity is the higher the rating

You then pick how many days a week you want to do this habit. We then multiply the difficulty (rating * days) a week to get your weekly target

Everytime you do the habit you tick the box for the that day. When you reach your weekly goal the row will go green
